Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?

Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your bike—not just “riding” your bike. This will help you be a more focused and legally compliant bike rider.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?

Always follow traffic rules, use bike lanes, and signal your turns. Wear a helmet, stay visible, and be alert to your surroundings.

My bike has been in storage is it safe to ride?

Before riding, inspect your bike for safety by checking the tires, brakes, chain, and overall frame condition. If it’s been in storage for a long time, consider a professional tune-up to ensure everything is in working order.

What rules should I follow when riding my bike?

Follow traffic laws, ride in the same direction as traffic, and use hand signals for turns. Wear a helmet, stay alert, and ensure your bike is equipped with lights or reflectors for visibility.
